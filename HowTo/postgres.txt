Lokale Installation neuer postgres Server vom zip-File
==========================
Download: https://www.enterprisedb.com/download-postgresql-binaries

Tools zur Installation liegen unter $env:OneDrive\Kurt\Dev-Infos\postgres-howto

Installation:
& $env:OneDrive\Kurt\Dev-Infos\postgres-howto\Kurt\Dev-Infos\postgres-howto\prepare_postgres.ps1 -new_postgres_directory v16 -postgres_zip_package .\postgresql-16.3-1-windows-x64-binaries.zip

Start/Stop:
Start_Database_Manually.bat / Stop_Database_Manually.bat
